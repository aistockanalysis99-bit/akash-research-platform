Overview

What We Are Building

A complete AI-driven portfolio management system that combines a battle-tested quantitative momentum strategy with a layer of intelligent AI agents — inspired by how real hedge funds operate, but built for a fraction of the cost.

The system scans the S&P 100 every evening, identifies high-probability momentum breakouts, submits them to an AI review panel, and manages a virtual portfolio of up to 20 positions with daily monitoring, exit logic, and full reasoning transparency.

💡 Key Principle: The quantitative engine finds the signals. The AI decides which ones to take, how big, and when to exit — considering the full portfolio, market regime, news, and fundamentals at every step.

📊

Quantitative Foundation

Vol-normalized multi-horizon momentum across 1, 3, 6, and 12-month returns. Rules-based, backtested, zero look-ahead bias.

🤖

AI Verification Layer

Multi-agent panel reviews every signal: analyst, bull/bear debate, risk manager, and portfolio manager — before any position is taken.

🗂️

Active Portfolio Management

Every morning, AI reviews all open positions. Exits flagged when thesis breaks. Portfolio-level decisions on concentration and exposure.

📱

Telegram Communication

Every decision — entry, hold, exit, rejection — delivered with full AI reasoning. Morning briefings and evening signal alerts.

AI Layer

The AI Agent Team

Inspired by the TradingAgents open-source framework — adapted to work with our momentum signal engine rather than generating signals from scratch. Every entry decision goes through the full panel.

📰

Analyst Agent

Information Gathering

Pulls latest news, earnings dates, revenue trends, and analyst ratings. Produces a structured market context brief for the symbol.

🐂

Bull Researcher

Long Thesis

Reads the analyst brief and builds the strongest possible case for taking the trade. Identifies catalysts and tailwinds.

🐻

Bear Researcher

Counter-Thesis

Builds the opposing view — risks, overvaluation concerns, macro headwinds, regulatory risks. Forces the system to see both sides.

⚖️

Risk Manager

Portfolio Safety

Checks position sizing, portfolio exposure, sector concentration, correlation with existing positions. Flags if risk limits are near.

🏦

Portfolio Manager

Final Decision

Reads all agent outputs + full portfolio context. Makes the final call: APPROVE, REJECT, or RESIZE with written reasoning.

🌅

Morning Reviewer

Daily Monitoring

Reviews all open positions daily at 08:00 ET. Checks if original thesis still holds. Flags exits before they become losses.

🔑 Key difference from TradingAgents: Their agents generate signals from scratch. Ours only act on signals the quantitative engine has already approved — adding judgment on top of math, not replacing it.

AI Cost Design — Under $30/Month

The system uses a tiered multi-provider model — the cheapest AI that can handle each task, with more capable (and expensive) models reserved for complex decisions only.

Task AI Model Calls/Month Est. Cost
Morning portfolio review (all positions in 1 call) Gemini 2.0 Flash Free tier 22 $0
Signal analyst brief (news + fundamentals) Gemini 2.0 Flash Free tier ~110 $0–$1
Bull / Bear debate per signal DeepSeek V3 $0.27/M ~110 $1–$3
Portfolio Manager final decision Claude Haiku / DeepSeek $1/M ~132 $3–$8
Weekly portfolio strategy review Claude Sonnet / Gemini Pro rare use 4 $1–$3
Total estimated monthly AI cost ~270 calls $5–$15

* Gemini 2.0 Flash free tier: 1,500 requests/day. We use ~12/day. Cost = $0 until 10× current volume. DeepSeek V3 and Qwen 2.5 (Chinese providers) offer high reasoning quality at very low cost.

Operations

What Happens Every Trading Day

Fully automated. No manual intervention required.

08:00 — FMP Data Pull

Incremental refresh of latest prices for all 100 stocks and open positions.

08:10 — AI Portfolio Review

Single batch call reviews all open positions. HOLD / WATCH / EXIT per position.

08:25 — Telegram Briefing

Morning summary: portfolio P&L, any exits flagged, positions to watch today.

🌆 Evening Session (16:30–17:00 ET)

16:30 — EOD Data + Signal Scan

Momentum engine runs on today's close. Identifies breakout candidates.

16:35 — AI Agent Review

Each candidate: analyst brief → bull/bear → portfolio manager decision.

16:50 — Telegram Signals

Approved entries delivered with AI reasoning. Virtual portfolio updated.
